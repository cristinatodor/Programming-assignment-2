About the city

With increasing flight links to European cities, Cluj (as it's called for short) is welcoming more and more travellers. Along with fine dining, excellent cultural activities, a wonderful historical legacy and a great atmosphere, the city will not disappoint those who add it to their travel itinerary. What's more is the fact it is so easy to access and get around.

Location

Cluj-Napoca is located in Transylvania, Central Romania and is the capital of Cluj County.
